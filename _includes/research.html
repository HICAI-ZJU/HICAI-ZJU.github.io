Research

The team is committed to explore Knowledge Graphs, Large Language Models and other modern AI technologies to advance scientific discovery.

Knowledge Graphs

The Knowledge Graphs (KGs) models world knowledge in a graph-like form. It plays important roles in representing knowledge in many scientific domains such as Biology, Chemistry, etc.

Large Language Models

The Large Language Model (LLMs) are deep learning models pre-trained on very large-scale corpus, and typically general purpose models that excel at a wide range of tasks.

AGI for Science

Modern AI technologies are believed to have the potential of transforming the way of scientific discovery.
