Research

The team is committed to explore Knowledge Graphs, Large Language Models and other modern AI technologies to advance scientific discovery.

Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge Graphs (KGs) model world knowledge in graph-like forms, and have been widely deployed in modeling scientific knowledge in life sciences, drug discovery,Chemistry etc.

Large Language Models

Large Language Model (LLMs) such as chatGPT are deep neural network models pre-trained on large-scale corpus, typically with a general purpose that excels at a wide range of tasks.

AGI for Science

Modern AI technologies are believed to have the potential of transforming the way of scientific discovery. AI-powered copilots would enable more efficient scientific work.
