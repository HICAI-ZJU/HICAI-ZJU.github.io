Research

The team is committed to explore Knowledge Graphs, Large Language Models and other modern AI technologies to advance scientific discovery.

Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge Graphs (KGs) model world knowledge in graph-like forms, and have been widely deployed in modeling scientific knowledge in life sciences, drug discovery, chemistry etc.

Large Language Models

Large Language Model (LLMs) such as chatGPT are deep neural models pre-trained on very large-scale corpus or data, typically with a general purpose that excels at a wide range of tasks.

AGI for Science

AI-powered robots or copilots are likely to transform science and fundamentally alter the creative processes by which scientist formulate ideas and put them into experiments.
