This team, led by Prof. Huajun Chen and Dr. Qiang Zhang, is mainly dedicated to AI advancements in cross-disciplinary applications to biology, chemistry, materials and drugs.

The Team Leaders

Huajun Chen

Professor

Prof. Huajun Chen is a professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang University . His main research directions are knowledge graphs, big data systems, natural language processing, etc. He have Published many papers in top international conferences or journals such as WWW, IJCAI, AAAI, KR, ACL, EMNLP, KDD, VLDB, ICDE, ACM MM and TKDE. He has presided over several national natural Science foundations He Won ISWC2006 Best Paper Award (first work), the first prize of Technology Invention of the University Science and Technology Award, the first prize of Qian Weichang Chinese Information Processing Technology Award, the Alibaba Outstanding Academic Cooperation Award, China Industry and Information Technology Media Publishing and the first prize of the group's outstanding publication.

Qiang Zhang

Assistant Professor

Dr. Qiang Zhang is a Principal Investigator under the Hundred Talents Program at Zhejiang University. Before joining Zhejiang University, he was a postdoctoral research fellow in machine learning working with Prof. Emine Yilmaz, affiliated with the Centre for Artificial Intelligence in University College London (UCL). He studied for the Ph.D. in UCL as well. Prior to that, he obtained M.Sc. from Chinese Academy of Sciences and B.Sc. from Shandong University. His research interests lie in the area of machine learning and span the entire theory-to-application spectrum from fundamental advances (e.g., Bayesian inference and meta learning) to practical progress (e.g., text mining, sequential data modeling). His research has been applied to text classification, knowledge graphs, information retrieval, recommender systems, and misinformation detection. Recently, he has been mainly focusing on AI applications to scientific discovery.
